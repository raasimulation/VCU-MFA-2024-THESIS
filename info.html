Information about 2024 MFA Thesis Exhibition

The 2024 MFA Thesis Exhibition features 33 outstanding emerging artists and designers. Representing eight departments, these students’ ambitious and challenging research pushes beyond traditional disciplines to shape what is new and next in visual and material culture. 

The MFA Thesis Exhibition is held at The Anderson (907 ½W Franklin St, Richmond) and takes place in two rounds:

Round 1: April 5–April 19, Opening Reception: April 5, 5–8 p.m. 
Round 2: April 26–May 11, Opening Reception: April 26, 5–8 p.m.
